Word Lok

how to play

find all letters in the word in 11 moves.

  • You have only 5 seconds to memorize the position of the word's letter on the grid before it is hidden
  • Once the grid is hidden, your task is to click on the squares of the grid that correspond to the correct position of each letter in the word
  • You only have 11moves; meaning you can only click on the grid squares 11 times before the game ends
  • If you select the correct squares for a letter, it will be marked as found and stay open
  • If you select the wrong squares for a letter, it won't be marked and will close
  • Once you have located all the letters of the word before the 11 moves is up, You have successfully completed the round and can move on to the next game!
  • If you can't find all the letters in the word before the 11 moves is up, then you failed and can try again
